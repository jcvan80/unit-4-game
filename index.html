Crystal Collector

Insturction on how to play the game.


  1. You'll be given a random number at the beginning of the game."Number To Achieve"
  2. There'll be four crystals below.
    • Blue
    • Green
    • Red
    • Yellow
  3. By click a crystal , you will be add a specific amount of number to your total score.
  4. You win the game by correctly getting the specific amount of points to your total score.
  5. You lose the game if you go over your total score.
  6. The value of each crystal is not known until you click on the crystal.
  7. Each time the game starts your total score and the value of each crystal will change.
